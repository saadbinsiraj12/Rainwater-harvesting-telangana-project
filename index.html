About Us

AquaHarvest is a pioneering initiative dedicated to promoting rainwater harvesting across Telangana. Founded in 2020, our mission is to combat water scarcity through innovative and sustainable solutions.

With years of expertise in water conservation, our team of engineers and environmentalists have designed customized rainwater harvesting systems that cater to the unique geographical and climatic conditions of Telangana.

We believe that every drop counts, and our systems ensure that not a single drop of precious rainwater goes to waste. From residential buildings to large commercial complexes, we provide end-to-end solutions for all your water conservation needs.

Our vision is to make Telangana a water-secure state by empowering communities with the knowledge and tools to harness rainwater effectively.
